Other Stuffs!

Welcome to my "Other Stuffs" page. It is currently under-construction. At some point I hope to use this page as a way to display a bit of ✨sparkle✨, a little bit of 💫 personality 💫....so you know, hobbies and other interests.

In all seriousness, I think it is critical to have hobbies. Some days you are going to try to make research progress and nothing will go according to plan. A term will be dropped in a long proof, a single missing character will cause your whole program to crash, and you will just want to quit. It is the ability to push past the inevitable frustration that comes with trying something new that will determine a student's ability to succeed. So when you want to quit, do other stuffs instead. Do other stuffs just barely long enough until you forget why you wanted to quit, and then keep doing research! It is a flawless algorithm. Nothing could possibly go wrong. Especially not burn out.
